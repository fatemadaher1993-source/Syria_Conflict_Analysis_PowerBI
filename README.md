## ğŸ¯ Project Goal

To move beyond simple event counting by establishing a **Conflict Severity Index** (Lethality Index) to better inform resource allocation for protection programming and transitional risk management in Syria.

## ğŸ’¡ Key Analytical Findings

1.  **Extreme Lethality Risk in Tartous:** The analysis found that Tartous records the highest **Fatalities per Event (FPE)** of **3.74**, requiring specialized, targeted protection protocols.
2.  **Disparity in Risk Profiles:** **Idlib** faces a high **Frequency Risk** (most events), while **Deir-ez-Zor** faces a high **Impact Risk** (most fatalities).
3.  **Transitional Risk:** Identified the potential for post-conflict **vengeance cycles and sectarian violence** in high-impact areas, necessitating immediate Community-Based Conflict Resolution programs.
